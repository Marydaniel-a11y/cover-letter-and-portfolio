Hi Reader.

I'm Mary a product designer based in Tel Aviv, passionate about this beautiful new intersection of AI products software, hardware, and us humans. Over the past few years, I've helped launch and refine digital products at companies like Lightricks and Wix, from early discovery through to production.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me at Beit Hanna working out, covered in clay at a ceramics class, playing the flute, or planning my next hiking trip with my smart, funny, and wonderful husband, Roi.

I also make a homemade sour cherry liqueur (Vishnya) that I'm very proud of a small tradition inspired by my amazing grandmother, Lorika. And one day, I'll finally find the time to go snowboarding again and (hopefully!) earn my second star in scuba diving this time without the ear infection.

At Lightricks, I was part of a team that built a new AI-based product from the ground up, using tools like Figma Make, Bolt, Cursor, Claude, and ChatGPT to ideate and iterate faster. That experience deepened my curiosity for how AI can empower creativity and simplify complexity.

I'm especially excited about the opportunity to learn, grow, and help shape foundational AI experiences that feel intuitive, empowering, and seamlessly integrated into users' daily workflows. I believe my strengths lie in my diligence, curiosity, broad perspective, and strong communication skills especially when working closely with developers and data teams to bring ideas to life.
